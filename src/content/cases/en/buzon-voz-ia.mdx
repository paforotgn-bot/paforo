## The challenge

A high-volume physiotherapy clinic was losing over 20 calls a day. Receptionists couldn't keep up: while attending patients at the front desk, the phone rang non-stop. Frustrated patients hung up, many never called back, and appointments, urgent cancellations and new clients were being lost.

## The solution

### Ultra-human AI answering machine

We designed a voicemail system with a natural, empathetic voice that greets the patient, explains that they can't be attended right now and invites them to leave a message. The tone is so natural that patients leave detailed messages without feeling like they're talking to a machine.

### Automatic AI transcription

Every voice message is automatically transcribed using artificial intelligence. Receptionists see a clean, clear dashboard showing: time, phone number, patient name and the full transcribed message.

### Direct WhatsApp response

From the dashboard itself, with a single click on the "WhatsApp" button, the receptionist opens a direct conversation with the patient to confirm an appointment, answer their query or manage a cancellation. No need to return calls, no wasted time.

## The results

The clinic went from losing over 20 daily calls to handling 100% of requests. Receptionists save more than 2 hours a day that were previously spent returning calls and managing messages. Patients receive a quick response and satisfaction has increased notably.

Most importantly: every missed call was a dissatisfied patient or an appointment that never materialised. Now, no call goes unanswered.
